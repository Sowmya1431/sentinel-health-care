About the Project

Your AI-powered hospital monitoring and safety partner.

Sentinel Health is an AI-based hospital monitoring system that detects falls, analyzes patient behavior, and tracks hospital activity. Every user and device has a secure **unique ID** to ensure complete safety and privacy.

Once a video is uploaded, the system auto-analyzes it and provides: alerts, risk detection, staff logs, and visual analytics.
